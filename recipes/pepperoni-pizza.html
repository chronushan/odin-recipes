Pepperoni Pizza

Go back

Pepperoni is a variety of spicy salami made from cured pork and beef seasoned with paprika and chili peppers. Prior to cooking, pepperoni is characteristically soft, slightly smoky, and bright red. Sliced pepperoni is one of the most popular pizza toppings in American pizzerias.

The pepperoni pizza is one of my favorite pizza. I love meat and while the pizza might be simple, the simplicity is what makes it delicious.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup water
  • ½ (12 ounce) can CONTADINA Tomato Paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil, crushed
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220 degrees.
  2. Make sauce.
  3. Make crust.
  4. Knead the dough.
  5. Divide the dough. Top the dough with the sauce and the toppings.
  6. Bake the pizza.
